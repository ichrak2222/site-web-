About Us

Welcome to the Beauty World!

Parfumerie Slah was created in 2016. We specialize in the sale of perfumery, cosmetic and care products for individuals and professionals in the field of hairdressing, aesthetics and Spa.
We market different ranges of medium and high-end products of impeccable quality, original products that meet your expectations and are within reach of all budgets.
Our staff is continuously trained on our products in order to offer you the best advice to best meet your needs. Your satisfaction is our priority.
